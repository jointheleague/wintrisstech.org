Roomba Robot Contest


Wintriss Technical Schools (WTS) will be holding the first international Autonomous Roomba Robot Contest (iARRC) on November 18th in San Diego. Students from WTS are using Sun SPOTs to control their Roombas, however any autonomous control system will be acceptable.




The contest will require navigation around obstacles to find and home in on a Roomba battery charger station. Cash prizes, Roombas and SPOTs will be awarded to winners. Check this page often or email us a request for more information.
